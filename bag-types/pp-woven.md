# PP Woven Bags

Basic non-laminated polypropylene woven bags used for bulk industrial
and agricultural packaging.

This is the most widely used bag type globally.

---

## What it is
PP woven bags are made from polypropylene tapes woven into fabric.
They are breathable, lightweight, and cost-effective.

They do not have any moisture barrier unless laminated.

---

## Typical Uses
- Grains (rice, wheat, maize)
- Gypsum powder
- Cement raw materials
- Minerals and ores
- Fertilizers (non-hygroscopic)
- Sugar and salt

This bag is preferred where moisture protection is not critical.

---

## Common Specifications
- Material: Polypropylene (PP)
- Bag type: Non-laminated
- Capacity: 25 kg / 40 kg / 50 kg
- Printing: None or 1–2 color
- Closure: Heat cut or hemmed top
- Bottom: Single or double fold stitching

---

## GSM Range Guide
- 60–70 GSM: Light-duty grains
- 75–85 GSM: Gypsum, cement materials
- 90–100 GSM: Fertilizers, minerals

GSM selection depends on product density and handling conditions.

---

## Advantages
- Lowest cost bag option
- Easy to manufacture and source
- Breathable (reduces condensation)
- High availability worldwide
- Easy compression for container loading

---

## Limitations
- No moisture protection
- Not suitable for hygroscopic products
- Printing quality is basic
- Can weaken if exposed to rain or water

---

## Container & Packing Notes
- Usually packed in compressed bales
- 40 FT HC container: ~18,000–22,000 bags
- Volume fills before weight
- Bale compression directly affects profitability

Always confirm bale size and bags-per-bale before quoting.
